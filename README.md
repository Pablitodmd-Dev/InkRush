## Project Overview

**InkRush** is a high-speed, frenetic arcade experience. The concept centers on a "Cursed Sketchbook" where doodles have come to life and are bored. To keep them contained (and prevent them from erasing reality), the player must complete a series of absurd, short-lived challenges. You have 5 seconds per task. Fail, and the ink spills!

### Key Features
* **⚡ Frenetic Chaos:** Micro-games that last exactly 5 seconds. Tests reflexes and mental agility to the limit.
* **✏️ Scribble Aesthetic:** Hand-drawn, doodle-style visuals that provide a charming, chaotic, and authentic look.
* **👹 Boss Battles:** Every set number of points, the player faces a Boss Stage. Winning the battle increases the base difficulty level for all subsequent micro-games.
* **❤️ Survival Mode:** The player starts with 4 lives. Every mistake is costly.

### Game Modes
* **📖 Story Mode:** Progress through the sketchbook by completing micro-games and reaching a specific target score to "finish" the story.
* **♾️ Endless Mode:** A pure survival challenge; play indefinitely until all 4 lives are lost, aiming to climb the high-score leaderboard.

### Game Structure & Difficulty
The game operates on a scaling loop that ramps up in intensity as the score increases.

| Phase | Action | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal Loop** | Complete micro-games | Speed increases gradually with each success. |
| **Boss Event** | Defeat the Boss Minigame | **Victory:** Increases the "Complexity Tier" (speed/difficulty) permanently. |
| **Game Over** | Lose all 4 lives | Game ends; final score is displayed. |
